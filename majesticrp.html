About the Project

Two unofficial companion applications designed for the Majestic RP community. Each serves a distinct purpose: Search Maj is a general-purpose law and regulation lookup tool, while SANG Helper is tailored specifically for the in-game military service known as SANG. Both applications share a common DNA — speed, clarity, and ease of use — but express it through different visual identities.

🔍 Search Maj

The logo was crafted by extracting the "Maj" portion from the original Majestic RP logo and combining it with a search-oriented symbol. The bright green color (#15FF00) defines the brand — energetic, digital, and instantly recognizable. The UI is clean and minimal, allowing players to quickly search through game laws, rules, and documentation without friction.

⚡ SANG Helper

Built specifically for the SANG military service within Majestic RP, this tool provides specialized search functionality for service-related regulations, protocols, and internal documentation. Its identity is anchored in vibrant yellow (#FFCF0F), distinguishing it from Search Maj while maintaining a cohesive family feel. The interface is optimized for quick in-character lookups during active roleplay scenarios.

Design Philosophy

Both applications operate on a dark gray background (#1a1a1a), ensuring excellent readability while reducing eye strain during extended gaming sessions. The color-coded system — green for general search, yellow for SANG-specific — creates intuitive visual separation while keeping the tools within the same ecosystem. Typography is bold and functional, prioritizing legibility at a glance.
