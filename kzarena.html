About the Project

KZ-Arena is a cutting-edge computer club and gaming arena that demanded a visual identity as bold and energetic as its community. The design centers around a powerful flame logo — alive, dynamic, and unforgettable. The flame features stylized eyes that give it personality, watching over the competitive space like a guardian of the game.

The orange and white color palette was chosen to evoke heat, energy, and clarity. Orange represents the fire of competition and passion for gaming, while white brings balance, modernity, and a clean digital aesthetic. Together, they create a striking contrast that works across all touchpoints — from screens and merchandise to environmental signage.

The typography is bold and geometric, reinforcing the club's modern, tech-forward positioning. Every element of the identity system — from the logo's fiery contours to the supporting graphic patterns — was designed to ignite excitement and create a memorable brand experience for gamers.
