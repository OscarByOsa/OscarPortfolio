About the project

A minimalist Jupiter candy anchors the identity — playful, precise, never heavy. No gloom, just cheeky energy and a dancefloor pulse.

The logo lives in a rough cut-out text aesthetic. Letters feel like mismatched scraps: slightly shifted, alive, torn in rhythm. Only white and violet hold the space. Sterile glow meets electric depth.
